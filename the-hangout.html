The Hangout Website

Audience: Teens and young adults living with cystinosis.

Project: The Hangout website was built as a companion to CystinosisUnited.com, specifically tailored to address the unique needs of teenagers and young adults living with cystinosis. This website is designed to be an engaging space where the younger cystinosis community can not only find valuable information about their condition but also prepare for the responsibilities that come with growing up.

Timeframe: 2 months

Technologies: HTML CSS Bootstrap JavaScript CrownPeak CMS
