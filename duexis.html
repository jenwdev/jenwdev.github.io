DUEXIS Website

Audience: Patients and caregivers of patients who use DUEXIS.

Project: The DUEXIS website underwent a complete reconstruction, transitioning from a multipage format to a single-page site. This transformation has resulted in a user-friendly and comprehensive resource, specifically designed to furnish patients with detailed information about DUEXIS for their wellness journey. The new layout ensures clarity and simplicity, presenting everything patients require to understand the product on a single, easy-to-navigate page.

Timeframe: 1 month

Technologies: HTML SCSS Node Bootstrap JavaScript CrownPeak CMS
