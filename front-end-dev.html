Phone Number Swap

Background/Problem: The phone number listed on the Outpatient Care landing pages was pointing to the marketing contact center that had service hours of 8am-5pm. Calls outside the service hours were getting lost because there was no voice mail option. Marketing wanted to keep the contact center number during service hours and requested a solution.

Resolution: Wrote a script to swap phone numbers to the Outpatient Care locations phone numbers when the contact center was closed.
