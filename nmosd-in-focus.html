NMOSD In Focus Website

Audience: Health care providers and patients of NMOSD, neuromyelitis optica spectrum disorder, in the EU.

Project: NMOSD in Focus is a multi-lingual international website crafted exclusively for healthcare providers and patients dealing with Neuromyelitis Optica Spectrum Disorder (NMOSD). This disease awareness site has been developed with a European audience in mind, aligning seamlessly with EU standards by omitting any product-related content. Available in English for EMEA and in native languages for Germany, Spain, Italy, and France, NMOSD in Focus serves as a vital resource hub launched during NMOSD Awareness Month.

Timeframe: 3 months

Technologies: HTML CSS Bootstrap JavaScript CrownPeak CMS
